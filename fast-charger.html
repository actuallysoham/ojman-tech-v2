Fast Charging Station

Description

Ojman’s EVC series Battery Chargers are compact and rugged units designed using latest MOSFET based soft switching power conversion technology. These products are designed for applications in road transport vehicles for onboard and off-board battery charging. The hardware architecture and electronics packaging complies with severe external temperature requirement. The units are housed in IP67 casings and can be installed inside any road transport vehicle.
The battery charger is fed by 1Φ AC voltage input and operates at high switching frequency to generate regulated voltage at the output. An isolated AC/DC converter creates the required voltage and current for the Battery. A microprocessor based control circuit monitors battery voltage and current and creates PWM pulses for the inverter.
The output current for battery charging is measured and is limited to the maximum allowable battery charging current.
